Lamisil Oral Side Effects

Serious Side Effects of Lamisil

Some side effects can be serious, talk to your doctor immediately if you experience any of these side effects.
  • Fatal liver disease symptoms, such as dark urine, yellowing eyes and skin, persistent nausea and vomitting
  • Severe dizzy spells
  • Difficulty breathing

Common Side Effects of Lamisil

  • Fever related chills
  • Vision changes

Warnings & Recalls for Lamisil Oral

No Black Box Warnings have been recorded for this drug.

Lamisil Oral Treatment and Use

Lamisil Oral | Terbinafine, is used in the treatment of fungal infections in the finger or toe nail area. It is an oral medication. Lamisil is an anti-fungal medication. It can also be used to treat athlete’s foot and jock itch.

How Does Lamisil Work?

This oral medication works by stopping the increase or growth of fungal infections.

 Dosage Information

This medication is prescribed under certain conditions, and therefore it is best to let your doctor decide what dosage is right for you.
