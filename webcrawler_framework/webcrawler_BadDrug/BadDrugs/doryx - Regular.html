Doryx Side Effects

Doryx | Doxycycline Hyclate may cause side effects. The adverse effects of Doryx | Doxycycline Hyclate are:
  • diarrhea
  • itching of the rectum or vagina
  • sore mouth
Some side effects can be serious:
  • severe headache
  • blurred vision
  • skin rash
  • hives
  • difficulty breathing or swallowing
  • redness of the skin (sunburn)
  • yellowing of the skin or eyes
  • itching
  • dark-colored urine
  • light-colored bowel movements
  • loss of appetite
  • upset stomach
  • vomiting
  • stomach pain
  • extreme tiredness or weakness
  • confusion
  • decreased urination
If you or a loved one has experienced serious adverse effects from this drug, contact our advocates right away.

Warnings & Recalls for Doryx

Doryx | Doxycycline Hyclate has the potential to be contraindicated in patients who’ve got a hypersensitivity to its component ingredients.  Tell your doctor if you’re allergic to Doxycycline Hyclate.  You should also know that there have been reports of benign intracranial hypertension in adults receiving tetracyclines.  If you’re pregnant or nursing, you should be aware of this drug as there have been instances of bulging fontanels being reported in infants receiving tetracyclines and there have been cases where fibula growth rates have impeded in infants.Avoid concomitant use with penicillins.As of yet there haven’t been any recalls initiated by either the manufacturers of this drug or one by the FDA.If you have been impacted negatively by this drug, contact our advocates right away.

Doryx Treatment and Use

Doryx | Doxycycline Hyclate is a drug that’s used to treat certain bacterial infections such as:

  • Pneumonia
  • Respiratory tract infections
  • Lyme disease
  • Acne
  • Other skin infections
  • Genital infections
  • Urinary infections
  • Anthrax after inhalational exposure
  • Malaria

Doryx | Doxycycline Hyclate is in a classification of medications called tetracycline antibiotics and works by preventing the growth and spread of bacteria.  Doryx | Doxycycline Hyclate comes in the form of tablets that can be split or crushed or used in food such as applesauce.  Doryx | Doxycycline Hyclate is marketed by Warner Chilcott in the US.

Doryx | Doxycycline Hyclate is considered an essential drug in the War Against Terror as it relates to the treatment of anthrax.
