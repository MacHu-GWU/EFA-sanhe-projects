Perjeta Side Effects

Serious Side Effects

If you experience any of these serious side effects, seek medical attention immediately:
  • Heart attack, or cardiac dysfunction
  • Leukopenia
  • Hypersensitivity reaction
 Common Side Effects:
  • Anemia
  • Weight loss, decrease in appetite
  • Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea
  • Inflammatory disease of mucus
  • Rash, alopecia

Warnings & Recalls for Perjeta

There is a Black Box Warning for Perjeta | Pertuzumab

 
  • Exposure to pertuzumab while pregnant can result in birth defects, or the death of the fetus.

Perjeta Treatment and Use

What Is Perjeta?

Perjeta | Pertuzumab is an FDA approved medication used in conjunction with chemotherapy to slow or stop the spread of breast cancer tumors. There is no evidence that Perjeta, when used withÂ trastuzumab and docetaxel, will increase likeliness of survival, currently.

Perjeta | Pertuzumab is for patients who have not yet undergone chemotherapy, or other breast cancer treatments. Ask your doctor is Perjeta is right for you.

 

How It Works

Perjeta is supposed to work by sending signals to your cells, telling them to divide. Ultimately, along with other cancer treatments, Perjeta is supposed to slow down the spreading of cancer cells and tumors, by creating anti-tumor activity. When combined with chemotherapy, the percentage of time that patients lived without cancer was increased.

Dosing Information

Perjeta is administered through an IV. Consult your doctor about what dosing is right for you.
