Voraxaze Treatment and Use

What Is Voraxaze?

Voraxaze | Glucarpidase is an enzyme that is given to cancer patients to help breakdown cancer treatment medications more easily in the body. It is used to treat toxicity levels of methotrexate. It is not suggested for those with impaired renal function due to exposure to methotrexate.
