Hydroxycut Side Effects

Severe Side Effects of Hydroxycut

  • Consult your doctor if you notice yellowing of your skin tone or darker urine. This could mean that you are developing jaundice. Jaundice is often seen in liver disease, a serious side effect of Hydroxycut.
  • Seek medical attention for extended periods of increased heart rate, as this could be an indication of ensuing cardiovascular problems.
  • Seizures, dizziness, and blurred vision are additional side effects that you should be aware of.

Common Side Effects of Hydroxycut

  • Loss of appetite is common when taking Hydroxycut because it inhibits the desire to eat.
  • Dehydration is another common side effect because Hydroxycut trims your water weight. Be sure to drink lots of fluids during your diet regimen.
  • Users may often feel nauseated and fatigued when ingesting Hydroxycut. Vomiting, abdominal pain, diarrhea, and headaches are also the norm.

Warnings & Recalls for Hydroxycut

Hydroxycut Recall

  • In 2009, the FDA issued a warning about the hazards of Hydroxycut. These dietary supplements were purported to have caused multiple cases of liver damage, rhabdomyolysis (a form of muscle damage that can lead to kidney failure), and even death. After a recall, Hydroxycut was reissued with a new formula and is still being sold today.

General Warnings About Hydroxycut

  • It is important to talk to your doctor before starting any weight loss regimen. You should not take Hydroxycut if you are pregnant or plan on becoming pregnant.
  • There is currently not much evidence to show that Hydroxycut is effective in helping with weight loss. With a healthy diet and regular exercise, studies indicate that non-users can achieve weight loss results that are similar to those of Hydroxycut. People should thoroughly research Hydroxycut before purchasing and ingesting it.

Hydroxycut Treatment and Use

Hydroxycut is a popular over-the-counter dietary supplement that is marketed as a weight loss supplement. Hydroxycut, in combination with a healthy, low-carb diet and exercise, allegedly results in a dramatic reduction of total body mass over time. It is marketed by Iovate Health Sciences.

How does Hydroxycut work?

The manufacturer of Hydroxycut claims that it helps users lose weight by burning body fat, curbing appetite, and boosting physical energy levels.

Dosage

The dosage for Hydroxycut products varies depending on the actual supplement that is taken. Users will typically start the regimen by taking one capsule, three times per day (30 to 60 minutes before each meal time) for a week. They will gradually increase the number of capsules taken if they respond well to the program. Users of Hydroxycut should drink a full glass of water (at least eight fluid ounces) with each dosage.
