Dextroamphetamine Side Effects

Common Side Effects:
  • Dry mouth
  • Constipation
  • Loss of appetite
  • Shaking of the body
  • Restlessness
  • Change in sex drive or ability
 

Serious Side Effects:

  • Chest pain
  • Slow or difficult speech
  • Weakness or numbness of arm or leg
  • Mood changes
  • Believing untrue things
  • Feeling unusually suspicious of others
Contact your doctor immediately if you experience any of these symptoms

Warnings & Recalls for Dextroamphetamine

This drug can be habit forming. Do not take excessive doses or more often or for longer than you are prescribed. If you take too much, the power of the drug will weaken, and you will require more, as with any drug.

Dextroamphetamine Treatment and Use

Dextroamphetamine is used in part of a program of treatment which is used to control some symptoms of attention deficit hyperactivity disorder. ADHD symptoms include difficulty focusing, controlling actions, or even remaining still and quiet.Â Dextroamphetamine is also used in the treatment of sleeping disorder narcolepsy.

Dextroamphetamine is in a class of medications known as central nervous system stimulants. It works by changing amounts of certain natural substances that are found inside the brain.

 

Ask your doctor what dosage is right for you. The medication comes as a liquid, tablet and even an extended release capsule which is taken orally. You must take it 2 to 3 times a day, or as prescribed. It can be taken either with or without food, at your discretion. Do not take it in the evening because it will make it difficult for you to go to sleep.
