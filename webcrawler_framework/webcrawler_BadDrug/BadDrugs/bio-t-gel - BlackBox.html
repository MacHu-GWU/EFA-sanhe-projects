Bio-T-Gel Side Effects

Serious Side Effects of Bio-T-Gel

  • worsened symptoms of an enlarged prostate gland: urinating more frequently, trouble urinating, more urgency to urinate, or inability to control your bladder
  • decrease in the amount of sperm
  • swelling of the body, especially the ankles or feet which may be accompanied by heart failure (heart is unable to pump sufficient amounts of blood throughout the body)
  • swelling or tenderness of the breasts
  • difficulty sleeping or breathing correctly while sleeping (sleep apnea)
  • blood clotting in the legs with symptoms of aching or pain, and inflammation or irritation of the legs
Bio-T-Gel may cause serious adverse reactions if a woman or child comes in contact with it. See "Warnings and Recalls" tab for more information.

Common Side Effects of Bio-T-Gel

  • increase in acne
  • irritation of the skin at the area of application
  • high cholesterol
  • raise in the amount of red blood cells
 

Warnings & Recalls for Bio-T-Gel

Bio-T-Gel Contraindications

Bio-T-Gel is contraindicated in and should not be used by women or children. If a woman who is pregnant or breastfeeding comes in contact with Bio-T-Gel, this product can harm the fetus or nursing baby. These patients should not handle Bio-T-Gel. If Bio-T-Gel comes in contact with the skin of a woman or child, the area should be washed immediately and thoroughly with soap and water.Females who have been exposed to Bio-T-Gel may suffer from the following symptoms:
  • swollen or inflamed clitoris
  • increase in breakouts or acne
  • changes in hair on the body
If a child has come into contact with Bio-T-Gel, they may develop side effects of:
  • aggression
  • enlargement or swelling of the genitals
  • early puberty or an increase in pubic hair
  • increase in the desire to have sex
  • bone growth disorders

General Warnings

Do not use Bio-T-Gel if you are allergic to any ingredient in the medicine (including soy), have prostate cancer or breast cancer.To be sure that you may safely use Bio-T-Gel let your doctor know about any of the following you may have:
  • any allergies
  • cancer of the breast or prostate
  • an enlarged prostate
  • any problems urinating
  • heart disease or disorders
  • liver or kidney disease
  • sleep apnea or trouble breathing correctly during sleep
Bio-T-Gel may increase the risk of developing prostate cancer. Ask your doctor about the risks and benefits of using Bio-T-Gel.
Bio-T-Gel is a controlled substance and could potentially be subject to abuse. Do not give Bio-T-Gel to anyone other than for whom it was prescribed. If you have a history of drug abuse, ask your doctor if it is safe for you to use Bio-T-Gel.

Bio-T-Gel Interactions

Bio-T-Gel may interact with certain medications. Be sure to tell your doctor about any other drugs you may be taking, especially:
  • diabetes medicines or insulin
  • corticosteroids ("steroids")
  • drugs for reducing the clotting of blood

Bio-T-Gel Treatment and Use

Bio T-Gel | Testosterone Gel is a new testosterone containing gel marketed by BioSante Pharmaceuticals Inc. and approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration in February of 2012 for treating hypogonadism, or abnormally low levels of testosterone in men. Bio-T-Gel may benefit patients by reducing symptoms of:

  • inability to have sex
  • loss of desire to have sex
  • loss of strength
  • bone loss
Bio-T-Gel should be used by men who are over the age of 18 only.

How Does Bio-T-Gel Work?

Bio-T-Gel works by absorbing into the skin and blood-stream where it may raise and balance levels of testosterone in men. Bio-T-Gel accounts for healthy hormone amounts to prevent unwanted side effects which may be caused by low testosterone levels.

How to Use Bio-T-Gel

Bio-T-Gel should be applied to the skin only. Use Bio-T-Gel as directed by your doctor, do not use more or less than recommended. Bio-T-Gel should be administered only by the patient who has been prescribed the drug. Do not allow anyone else to touch or handle Bio-T-Gel.

Bio-T-Gel should only be applied to areas such as the upper arms, stomach, shoulder, or abdominal area that will be covered by a short sleeve shirt. Do not apply Bio-T-Gel to your genital area (penis or scrotum). Allow the gel to dry and avoid contact with others and the site of application.

Wash your hands immediately after using Bio-T-Gel.

Do not swim, shower, or apply water to the area for 5 hours after application. If others will be making skin contact with the applied area, be sure to wash the skin thoroughly with soap and water before.

Avoid direct heat sources, open flames or smoking until the gel has dried; it may be flammable.
