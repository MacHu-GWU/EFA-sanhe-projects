Avandamet Side Effects

Serious Side Effects:

 Avandamet may increase risk of serious heart problems, and increase the risk of a stroke or heart attack. Other symptoms include: 
  • Jaundice
  • Severe blistering
  • Chest pain
  • Rapid weight gain
  

Warnings & Recalls for Avandamet

Avandamet is not available to all Type 2 Diabetes patients, but only available to those who understand the serious risks involved. Ask your doctor what is right for you.

Avandamet Treatment and Use

Avandamet | Metformin and Rosiglitazone is a combination used to regulate blood sugar levels in patients with Diabetes, as well as to regulate or stop the liver from creating more sugar, and helps to make the body more receptive to the insulin that it naturally produces.

 

Avandamet | Metformin is used in the treatment of patients with Type 2 Diabetes who do not take a daily insulin injections, and is not used to treat Type 1 Diabetes.

 

Avandamet is not available to all patients. Ask your doctor is Avandamet is right for you.
