Protonix Side Effects

Protonix | pantoprazole side effects and adverse reactions include:
  • Diarrhea
  • Headache
  • Nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain
  • Low magnesium
  • Insomnia
  • Joint pain
Very rarely, the following Protonix | pantoprazole side effects may occur. Contact your doctor immediately if you experience these:
  • Palpitations
  • Anxiety
  • Watery stool
  • Muscle weakness; limp muscle sensation
  • Allergic reaction

Warnings & Recalls for Protonix

  • It has been observed that individuals that take proton pump inhibitors for extended periods of time are at increased risk of bone fractures.
  • Proton pump inhibitors might increase your risk of clostridium difficile-associated diarrhea (CDAD).
  • You may experience low levels of magnesium if you take Protonix or another PPI.
  • Women who are taking Protonix should avoid nursing, as it is not known whether or not this drug is passed through breast milk.

Protonix Treatment and Use

Protonix | pantoprazole is a member of a class of drugs known as proton pump inhibitors (ppiâ€™s) and is used to treat erosive esophagitis as well as other conditions that involve an excess of stomach acid. Protonix is currently marketed by Wyeth-Ayerst Laboratories.

Protonix | pantoprazole is a PPI and works by blocking the acid producing areas of the stomach, thereby decreasing the amount of acid that your stomach produces.

Protonix dosage

Protonix | pantaprazole ppi comes as a delayed-released tablet and is indicated for adults for up to 8 weeks of treatment. The safety of treatment with Protonix beyond 8 weeks has not been established. The safety of using this drug on children has not been established.

  • For treatment of erosive esophagitis associated with GERD, the recommended adult dose is 40 mg once a day for up to 8 weeks.
  • For maintenance therapy of the healing of erosive espophagitis, the recommended adult dose is 40 mg a day.
  • For the treatment of pathological hypersecretory conditions like Zollinger-Ellison Syndrome, the recommended adult dose is 40 mg, twice daily.
