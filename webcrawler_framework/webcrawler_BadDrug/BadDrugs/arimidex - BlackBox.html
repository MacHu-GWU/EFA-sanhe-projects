Arimidex Side Effects

Arimidex | Anastrozole has been shown to cause side effects such as
  • nausea
  • vomiting
  • loss of appetite
  • stomach pain
  • weakness
  • headache
  • hot flashes
  • sweating
  • constipation
  • diarrhea
  • heartburn
  • weight gain
  • muscle, joint, or bone pain
  • breast pain
  • mood changes
  • depression
  • insomnia
  • anxiety
  • dizziness
  • vaginal bleeding
  • vaginal dryness or irritation
  • burning, pain, or tingling of the hands or feet
  • dry mouth
  • thinning of the hair

Warnings & Recalls for Arimidex

  • Arimidex has been shown to cause or worsen the effects of osteoporosis, and can increase the risk for bone thinning and broken bones.
  • Women with a prior history of ischemic heart disease have a possible increased risk for this disease when compared to others taking Arimidex.

Arimidex Treatment and Use

Arimidex | Anastrozole is a nonsteroidal aromatase inhibitor manufactured by AstraZeneca as an oral tablet. Arimidex works by decreasingÂ estrogen levels in the body, which effectively slows or halts the growth of certain types of breast cancer cells that feed on estrogen.

Arimidex | Anastrozole has been approved for the treatment of

  • Early stage breast cancer in women who have gone through menopause when used with other treatments, like surgery or radiation
  • Women who have early stage breast cancer that has spread, either within the breast or to other parts of the body
  • Breast cancer which has worsened after taking the medication tamoxifen
