Uroxatral Side Effects

Serious Side Effects:

If you experience any of these side effects, please call your doctor immediately.
  • Any type of rash
  • Swelling of the face or throat
  • Fainting
  • Chest Pains
Common Side Effects Include:
  • Dizziness
  • Sore throat
  • Fever
  • Constipation
 

Warnings & Recalls for Uroxatral

No Black Box Warnings have been recorded for this drug.

Uroxatral Treatment and Use

UroXatral | Alfuzosin is used to treat men suffering from symptoms of benign prostate hyperplasia. Symptoms of BPH include trouble urinating, or pain during urination, as well as frequent or urgent urination.

 

How it Works:

UroXatral is  a type of medication called  alpha blockers, which works as a relaxant to muscles in the prostate and bladder region, to help alleviate tension in the bladder, allowing urine to flow more easily. It comes as an oral tablet, with long lasting, fact acting effects. The pill is taken once a day.
