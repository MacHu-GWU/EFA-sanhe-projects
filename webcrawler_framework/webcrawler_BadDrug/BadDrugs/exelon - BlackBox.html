  • redness, itching, or swelling where the patch is placed
  • dizziness

Warnings & Recalls for Exelon

Patients who suffer from certain conditions should consult their healthcare physician before using Exelon to ensure that it is safe. These conditions include
  • liver disease
  • recent weight loss or appetite problems
  • heart rhythm disorders, such as slow heartbeats
  • stomach ulcer
  • enlarged prostate
  • seizure disorders, such as epilepsy
  • asthma or allergies
  • dyskinesia
Patients who are breastfeeding should consult their doctor before taking Exelon, as it is unknown whether this medication can be passed through breast milk to a nursing baby.
