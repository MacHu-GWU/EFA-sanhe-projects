Forfivo XL Side Effects

Serious Adverse Side Effects

Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride has some very serious adverse side effects associated with it, such as:
  • Cardiovascular side effects such as abnormal heartbeat and wide QRS complex, a disorder of the electrical activity of the heart
  • Dermatological side effects such as Stevens-Johnson syndrome, a deadly skin allergic reaction to the drug
  • Immunological side effects such as anaphylaxis, a deadly allergic reaction to the drug
  • Neurological side effects such as seizure
  • Psychiatric side effects such as exacerbation of depression, mania, psychotic disorders and suicidal thoughts

Common Adverse Side Effects

Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride has more common and less severe adverse side effects associated with it, such as:
  • Cardiovascular side effects such as high blood pressure and abnormal heartbeat
  • Dermatological side effects such as itching, rash and hives
  • Gastrointestinal side effects such as constipation and nausea
  • Musculoskeletal side effects such as joint pain and muscle pain
  • Neurological side effects such as confusion, dizziness, headache, insomnia and tremors
  • Otic side effects such as tinnitus, or confusion when it comes to hearing
  • Psychiatric side effects such as agitation, anxiety and hostile behavior
  • Reproductive side effects such as menstrual disorders
  • Respiratory side effects such as sore throat
  • Other side effects such as dry mouth

Warnings & Recalls for Forfivo XL

Black Box Warning

Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride has an associated “black warning” attached to it that denotes that antidepressants increase the risk of suicidal thinking and behaviors in children, teenagers and young adults in studies of major depression.

Contraindications

Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride is contraindicated in the following patients:
  • Patients who drink alcohol or use sedatives who are administered this drug are at a risk of seizures when they abruptly discontinue Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride
  • Patients with bulimia or anorexia are at a higher risk of seizures
  • Concurrent use with MAOIs or MAOI use within 14 days
  • Concurrent use with ticlopidine or clopidogrel
  • Concurrent use with other bupropion products
  • Concurrent use with antipsychotics, other antidepressants, theophylline or systemic steroids
  • Concurrent use with stimulants
  • Hypersensitivity to bupropion or any other component of the drug
  • Patients prone to seizures or who are epileptic

Precautions

Patients with the following preexisting conditions or issues probably should not use or be administered Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride:
  • Patients with psychiatric disorders that transcend major depression or seasonal affective disorder but whose psychiatric issues are manic
  • Patients who are suicidal
  • Patients who are addicted to opiates, cocaine or stimulants
  • Patients who drink alcohol
  • Patients who are diabetic
  • Patients with head injuries or central nervous system tumors
  • Patients with liver damage or impairment
  • Patients with high blood pressure
  • Patients with kidney disease or impairment

Pregnancy Warning

Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride has been given a pregnancy category grade of “C” by the FDA, entailing that it should be discontinued after knowledge of conception immediately as it poses a risk to the fetus.

Forfivo XL Treatment and Use

Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride is a drug that’s manufactured and marketed by Edgemont Pharmaceuticals in the US.  A new synthesis of the popular drug Bupropion, Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride is indicated for the treatment of depression, seasonal affective disorder and major depression.  For smokers who desire to quit smoking cigarettes, the synthesis of Bupropion known as Zyban is recommended as Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride is not recommended for people who wish to quit smoking.

Non-FDA labeled indications for this drug include treating bipolar disorder and sexual dysfunction due to abusing SSRIs.

Please refer to the “Warnings and Recalls” tab to get more information about the “black box” warning for Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride.

How This Drug Works

Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride is an aminoketone class antidepressant which weakly inhibits the neuron uptake of dopamine and norepinephrine.  It however does not inhibit monoamine oxidase or the reuptake of serotonin.

Dosage Information

Forfivo XL | Bupropion Hydrochloride comes as a 450mg pill to take once per day and can be taken with or without food.  The safety and efficacy of administering this drug to pediatric patients under the age of 18 has as of yet not been established.
