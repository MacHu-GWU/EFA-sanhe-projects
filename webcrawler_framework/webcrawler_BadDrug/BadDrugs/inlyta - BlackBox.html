Inlyta Side Effects

Serious Side Effects

There have been very few reported serious side effects, most of which were due to preexisting medical conditions. Serious Side Effects effected less than 1% in clinical trials. Talk to your doctor to find out if Inlyta is right for you.Inlyta | Axtinib has been approved for use in 2012 by the FDA 

Common Side Effects

The Common Side Effects of Inlyta include:
  • Constipation
  • Loss of appetite, loss of weight
  • Vomiting, diarrhea, and nausea
  • Hypertension
  • Hand-foot syndrome
 

Warnings & Recalls for Inlyta

There have been no reported recalls for Inlyta | Axtinib

Inlyta Treatment and Use

What is Inlyta?

Inlyta | Axtinib is a kinase inhibitor that works with the body to stop or slow down the growth of tumor and cancer progression. It was approved by the FDA in early 2012 for the treatment of advanced kidney cancer. Inlyta | Axtinib has also been successful in the treatment of breast cancer, as well as other tumor types.

Dosing

Inlyta | Axtinib is to be taken twice orally, with or without food, daily. Typical dosage is 5 mg, but consult with your doctor before starting Inlyta. It is advised that the drug be taken with a much water as possible, to ensure the expediency of effects.
