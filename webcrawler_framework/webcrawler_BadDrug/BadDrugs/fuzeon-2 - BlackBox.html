Fuzeon Side Effects

Serious Side Effects:

If you experience these side effects, call your doctor immediately-
  • Oozing, swelling, or severe pain in the injection spot
  • Pain, burning, or numbing of the feet or legs
  • yellow skin or eyes
  • Bloody urine
  • Swollen feet

Warnings & Recalls for Fuzeon

There are no Black Box Warnings recorded for this drug.

Fuzeon Treatment and Use

Fuzeon | Enfuvirtide Injection is used with other medicines to treat human immunodeficiency virus infection in patients not responding to other types of antiviral medication. Fuzeon is an HIV fusion inhibitor, a type of medication. Fuzeon does not prevent the spread of HIV to other people.

How it Works:

Fuzeon works by stopping the spread of HIV from infecting other cells which are not infected with the virus, by inhibiting their fusion with other cells.
