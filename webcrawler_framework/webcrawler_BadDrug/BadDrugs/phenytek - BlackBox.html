Phenytek Side Effects

Phenytek | phenytoin side effects and adverse reactions include the following. This is not an exhaustive list. Ask your doctor about the risks involved with taking Phenytek before you begin treatment with this medication.
  • slurred speech
  • rapid eye movement
  • problems with muscle coordination
  • confusion
  • fatigue
  • headache
  • nausea and vomiting
  • anxiety
  • fuller lips
  • gum swelling, tenderness, or malformation
  • excessive body hair

Warnings & Recalls for Phenytek

  • Tell your doctor about the following before taking Phenytek | phenytoin:
    • diabetes
    • liver disease
    • porphyria
    • swollen lymph nodes
    • if you are pregnant, nursing, or think you may be pregnant
    • allergies to any food or medication, even over the counter drugs
    • if you are taking any herbal supplements.
  • Do not take this medication together with antacids. Allow some time between taking these.
Do not consume large amounts of alcohol while taking this medication.

Phenytek Treatment and Use

Phenytek | phenytoin is an anticonvulsant drug that is used to control certain and prevent certain types of seizures. Phenytoin was one of the first seizure medications created (1938) and has been widely used since its inception. Phenytek is a once a day formulation of phenytoin that is used to help people with epilepsy control their seizures and was approved by the FDA in 2001.

Phenytek | phenytoin works by decreasing the abnormal electrical activity in the brain that is believed to cause seizures and seizure disorder. It is also used off-label to control irregular heartbeat as well as migraines.

Phenytek side effects

  • Phenytek side effects and adverse reactions include liver problems and worsening of the condition known as porphyria. For a more complete list of side effects, visit the “side effects” and “warnings” tabs above.

How to take Phenytek

  • Your doctor will give you instructions on how to take Phenytek. Follow these exactly. If you have any questions about how you should take Phenytek, be sure you ask your healthcare provider.
  • The starting adult dose of phenytoin is 100 mg. Phenytek doesn’t come in that dose, so usually another phenytoin containing medication will be used. The dose will then be raised to a maximum of 600 mg daily, though a dose this high is rare.
