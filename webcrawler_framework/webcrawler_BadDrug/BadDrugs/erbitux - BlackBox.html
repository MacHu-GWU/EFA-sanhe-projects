  • acne-like rash

  • pain, swelling, or reddening of the eyes
  • sensitivity to light
  • hair loss
  • cotton mouth
  • cracked lips
  • mouth sores
  • trouble swallowing
  • headache

  • insomnia
  • depression
  • nausea
  • constipation
  • stomach pain or heartburn
  • loss of appetite or weight loss
  • back pain
  • redness or swelling at the point of injection

Warnings & Recalls for Erbitux

Erbitux | cetuximab injection has earned a Black Box Warning for its risk of allergic reaction during the infusion period, as well as its risk for cardiopulmonary attacks, such as heart attack. Although the incidence of these side effects is low, doctors must still closely monitor patients' reactions to the medication.Erbitux has also received a Class C pregnancy label and should not be used if a patient is pregnant or breastfeeding. The side effects on an unborn child are unknown, and physicians are unsure what risks Erbitux could pose through breast milk transfer.
