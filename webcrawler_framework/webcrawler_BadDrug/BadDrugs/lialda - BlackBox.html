Lialda Side Effects

Lialda | Mesalamine has been shown to cause common side effect such as
  • nausea
  • vomiting
  • stomach cramps
  • diarrhea
  • gas
  • flu-like symptoms, such as fever or sore throat
  • constipation
  • headache
  • dizziness
  • fatigue
  • rash
More serious side effects of Lialda include:
  • severe stomach pain
  • cramping
  • fever
  • headache
  • bloody diarrhea

Warnings & Recalls for Lialda

Patients with the following conditions should consult their doctor before taking Lialda:
  • Pyloric stenosis
  • Allergy to sulfasalazine
  • Heart conditions, such as congestive heart failure
  • Kidney disease
  • Liver disease
Lialda has received a pregnancy Category B rating, and those who are pregnant or trying to become pregnant should consult their doctor before continuing use of this medication.Some patients using Lialda have reported serious adverse reactions such as:
  • Renal impairment
  • Mesalamie-induced acute intolerance syndrome
  • Hypersensitivity reactions
  • Hepatic impairment
  • Upper GI tract obstruction

Lialda Treatment and Use

Lialda | Mesalamine is an anti-inflammatory agent manufactured by Shire US, Inc. as an oral delayed-release tablet. It is unknown exactly how Lialda works, but it is thought to to decrease inflammation, treat diarrhea, and heal tissue damage by interfering with the production of a certain chemical in the body which causes these symptoms.

Lialda | Mesalamine has been indicated for the treatment of

  • Induction of remission in adults with active, mild to moderate ulcerative colitis
  • Maintenance of remission of ulcerative colitis

Lialda is not a cure for ulcerative colitis, and should only be used for the treatment of symptoms. For this medication to be effective, it must be used consistently.
