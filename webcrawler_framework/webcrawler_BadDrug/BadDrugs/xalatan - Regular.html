Xalatan Side Effects

Xalatan | latanoprost eye drops may cause side effects and adverse reactions. Though these are usually tolerable, some may be severe and in some instances merit discontinuation of the drug. The following is a list of possible Xalatan adverse reactions:
  • Burning sensation in the treated eye
  • Eyelid redness
  • Hyperemia
  • Keratitis
  • Darkening of the iris
  • Thickening of the eye lashes
Though rare, serious allergic reaction to latanoprost may occur. If you begin experiencing the following symptoms seek medical assistance immediately:
  • Rash/itchiness
  • Swelling
  • Trouble breathing
 

Warnings & Recalls for Xalatan

  • Xalatan | latanoprost eye drops may increase the brown pigment in treated eyes.
  • Use of this medication may thicken the eyelashes.
  • After prolonged use of Xalatan | latanoprost eye drops, the treated eye may look noticeably different.
If you have any questions about precautions pertaining to Xalatan | latanoprost eye drops, please speak to your physician.

Xalatan Treatment and Use

Xalatan | latanoprost is a drug that comes in eye-drop form made by the drug company Pfizer. Xalatan eye drops are indicated in the treatment of high eye pressure. High eye pressure, or ocular hypertension, is a condition that can damage the optic nerve and lead to glaucoma. Xalatan | latanoprost eye drops work to counter this effect and prevent significant loss of vision.

Xalatan is applied via an eye dropper, once daily in the evening, on the affected eye.
