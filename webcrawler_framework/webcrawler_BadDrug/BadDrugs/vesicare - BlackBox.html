Vesicare Side Effects

Vesicare | Solifenacin has been shown to cause side effects such as
  • dry mouth
  • dry eyes
  • blurred vision
  • mild constipation
  • nausea
  • stomach pain or upset stomach
  • dizziness
  • tiredness
  • flu-like symptoms, such as fever, sore throat, or body aches

Warnings & Recalls for Vesicare

Vesicare should not be taken by those who suffer from
  • urinary or gastric retention
  • uncontrolled narrow-angle glaucoma
  • allergies to solifenacin succinate
  • liver or kidney disease
  • long QT syndrome
Vesicare may cause side effects that can impair your vision and reaction time, and should be used carefully when driving.

Vesicare Treatment and Use

Vesicare | Solifenacin is an anticholinergic medication manufactured by Astellas Pharma as an oral tablet. Vesicare relaxes the muscles in the bladder and strengthens patients’ ability to control their urination.
