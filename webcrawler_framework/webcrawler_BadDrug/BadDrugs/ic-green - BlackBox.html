Ic Green Side Effects

Side effects of Ic Green

Ic Green has very few side effects and is generally not considered very toxic. Very rarely, patients who have not previously displayed allergies to iodides have had anaphlactic reactions to Ic Green. Please tell your doctor and whoever is administering your diagnostic test with Ic Green if you have had any allergic reactions or allergies to iodides. If you have experienced any adverse reactions to Ic Green that are not listed on this page, or if you would like to voice a negative experience you’ve had with this diagnostic testing agent, please fill our report a bad drug form.

Ic Green Treatment and Use

Ic Green | Indocyanine Green is a sterile, tricarbocyanine dye manufactured by Akorn, Inc. that is used in cardiac, circulatory, hapatic, and opthalmic  diagnostic testing. Indocyanine Green comes as a powder that contains 25 mg of the diagnostic agent mixed with no more than 5% of sodium iodine.

Ic Green is prepackaged with a sterile water for injection, with which the powder should be mixed and administered intraveniously.

How is Ic Green administered?

Ic Green is given intraveniously as an injection during diagnostic tests and only by trained medical personnel.
