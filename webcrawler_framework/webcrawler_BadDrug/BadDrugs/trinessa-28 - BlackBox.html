TriNessa 28 Side Effects

TriNessa 28 | ethinyl estradiol and norgestimate has been shown to cause side effects such as
  • nausea
  • vomiting
  • bloating
  • stomach cramps
  • breast tenderness or swelling
  • nipple discharge
  • freckling or darkening of the face
  • increased hair growth
  • loss of scalp hair
  • weight or appetite change
  • vaginal itching or discharge
  • changes in menstrual period
  • decreased sex drive

Warnings & Recalls for TriNessa 28

TriNessa 28 has been shown to cause an increased risk for:
  • high blood pressure
  • heart attack
  • stroke
  • blood clot
Women suffering from the following conditions should consult their doctor before using TriNessa 28:
  • heart disease
  • liver tumors
  • undiagnosed abnormal genital bleeding
Since TriNessa 28 is a contraceptive used to prevent pregnancy, it should not be taken by those who are pregnant, trying to become pregnant, or breastfeeding. This medication can be passed through breast milk, and may cause harm to unformed fetuses.

  • Changing the cervical mucus to interfere with the egg’s ability to be fertilized by sperm
