Caduet Side Effects

Caduet | amlodipine and atorvastatin has been shown to cause common side effects, such as:
  • headache
  • diarrhea
  • nausea
  • stomach pain
  • indigestion
  • slight muscle pain
  • joint pain
  • dizziness
  • flushed face
Caduet has also been shown to cause more serious side effects, such as:
  • muscle pain, tenderness, or weakness
  • fever
  • fatigue
  • dark colored urine
  • swelling
  • weight gain
  • urinating less than usual or not at all
  • sever drowsiness
  • feeling faint
  • quick, pounding heartbeat
  • increasing chest pain which spreads from the arm to the shoulder
  • nausea
  • sweating
  • upper stomach pain
  • itching
  • loss of appetite
  • clay-colored stools
  • jaundice
If you experience any serious side effects, or if symptoms persist, seek medical help immediately.

Warnings & Recalls for Caduet

Caduet has been shown to cause a rare but serious condition known as rhabdomyolysis which can cause kidney failure. If you have a history of renal impairment, alert your doctor so that you can be closely monitored to avoid this side effect.Caduet is contraindicted in patients who suffer from liver disease, or in patients who are pregnant, may become pregnant, or those who are breastfeeding.Myopathy may occur while taking Caduet, and patients who suffer from any unexplained muscle pain, tenderness, or weakness should alert their doctor immediately.Patients taking Caduet should avoid alcohol, and food that are high in fat or cholesterol, as these can interfere with the effectiveness of this medication.

Caduet Treatment and Use

Caduet | amlodipine and atorvastatin is an HMG CoA reductase inhibitor, or “statin”, medication manufactured by Pfizer as an oral tablet. Caduet uses two medications:

  • Lipitor | atorvastatin, a lipid-lowering agent that decreases LDL cholesterol and improves HDL cholesterol.
  • Norvasc | amlodipine, a calcium channel blocker whichÂ relaxes blood vessels, improves blood flow, and lessens the strain on the heart.

Caduet | amlodipine and atorvastatin is indicated for the treatment of the following:

  • High blood pressure
  • Angina
  • Coronary artery disease
  • To lower LDL cholesterol and triglycerides in the blood when combined with a low-fat diet

Caduet is also used to decrease the risk of certain conditions, such as heart attack, stroke, heart surgery, and chest pain in patients who suffer from heart disease or diabetes.
