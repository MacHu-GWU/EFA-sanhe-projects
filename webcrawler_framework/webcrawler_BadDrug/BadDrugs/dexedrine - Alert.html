Dexedrine Side Effects

Common Side Effects:
  • Restlessness
  • Dry Mouth or Unpleasant taste
  • Weight loss
  • Changes in sex drive or ability
 Serious Side Effects:
  • Chest pain
  • Slow or difficult speech
  • Weakness or numbness of an arm or leg
  • Frenzied or abnormally excited mood
  • Hostile behavior

Warnings & Recalls for Dexedrine

Dextraomphetamine can be habit forming. Do not take a larger dose, take it more often, or take it for a longer time than prescribed by your doctor. If you take too much Dexedrine, you may find the medication doesn't work as well as it should.Find out more from your doctor.

Dexedrine Treatment and Use

Dexedrine | Dextroamphetamine is used in the treatment of ADHD in adults and even children. It can also be used in the treatment of narcolepsy. Dexedrine is a medication known as a central nervous system stimulant. 

 

How it Works: 

Dexedrine works by changing the amount of natural substance found inside the brain, in order to help combat symptoms of ADHD and narcolepsy.
