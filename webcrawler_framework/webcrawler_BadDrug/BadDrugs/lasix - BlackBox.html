Lasix Side Effects

Serious Side Effects of Lasix

If you experience any of these side effects, call your doctor immediately-
  • Ringing in ears
  • Unusual bleeding or bruises
  • Peeling skin
  • Loss of hearing
  • Excessive weight loss

Common Side Effects of Lasix

  • Muscle cramp
  • Weak or dizzy feelings
  • Heavy, unquenchable thirst
  • Confusion

Warnings & Recalls for Lasix

No Black Box Warnings have been recorded for this medication

Lasix Treatment and Use

Lasix | Furosemide is a pill used in the reduction of swelling and fluid retention, which is caused by varying medical problems, which may include heart disease or liver disease. It can also be used in the treatment of high blood pressure.

How Does Lasix Work?

This medication causes the kidneys to release unnecessary water and salts from the body and into the urine. It comes in the form of a tablet, and liquid, which is taken orally, once or twice a day.

Dosage Information

Ask your doctor what dose is right for you. Do not take more than the prescribed amount of medication.
