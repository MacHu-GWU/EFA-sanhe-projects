Plendil Side Effects

Serious Side Effects:

Seek medical attention immediately if you experience these side effects.
  • Swelling of the face or other body parts
  • Difficult breathing
  • Development of a rash
 Common Side Effects: 
  • Fast heartbeat
  • Heartburn
  • Lightheadedness
  • Enlarged gum tissue near teeth

Warnings & Recalls for Plendil

No Black Box Warnings have been recorded for this drug.

Plendil Treatment and Use

Plendil | Felodipine is a medication used in the treatment of high blood pressure. It is a relaxant for your blood vessels, which allows your heart to relax and not work as hard.

 

How it Works:

Plendil comes in tablet form, taken orally, typically once a day. The medication relaxes your blood vessels, and allows blood to flow more easily. This relieves the stress that your heart takes on, and lowers the blood pressure.

 

Dosing Information: 

Ask your doctor what dosing is right for you.
