Serious Side Effects of Kenalog

Call your doctor immediately if you experience any of these side effects
  • Severe skin irritations
  • Skin infection
  • Difficulty breathing

Kenalog Treatment and Use

Kenalog | Triamcinolone Topical is used to treat itching, redness, dryness, crusting, scaling, and inflammation: various skin conditions.

How to Use Kenalog

Kenalog is a topical ointment, cream, or liquid which comes in varying strengths, used in the treatment of various skin conditions. It is often applied more than 3 times in a day. It is important to clean the necessary area before applying the treatment. Do not bandage or cover the area once you have applied the treatment, unless instructed to do so.

Keep away from eyes.

Dosage:

Ask your doctor what dosage is right for you.
