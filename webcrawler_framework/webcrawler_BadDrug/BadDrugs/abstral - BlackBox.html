Abstral Side Effects

Common Side Effects:
  • Unusual dreams
  • Dry mouth
  • Weight loss
  • Anxiety

Serious Side Effects

Consult your doctor should you experience any of these serious side effects:
  • Seizures
  • Hives
  • Itching
  • Rash
  • Fainting
  • Slow breathing or difficulty breathing
 

Warnings & Recalls for Abstral

No Black Box warnings have been recorded for this drug.

Abstral Treatment and Use

Abstral | Fentanyl is used in the treatment of breakthrough pain in cancer treatment of at least 18 years of age who are taking regular doses of other opiate pain medications. Abstral is in a class of medication called narcotic analgesics. It changes the method of brain and nervous system responses to pain.

It comes as a lozenge on a handle, a sublingual tablet, a film, or a buccal tablet. It is used as needed to treat the breakthrough pain of cancer patients. Talk to your doctor about how the medication should be used. Do not take more than prescribed by your doctor.
