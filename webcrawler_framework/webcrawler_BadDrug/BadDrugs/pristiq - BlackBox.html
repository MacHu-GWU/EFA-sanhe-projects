Pristiq Side Effects

Pristiq | Desvenlafaxine has been shown to cause such side effects as
  • increased sweating
  • dizziness
  • drowsiness
  • loss of appetite
  • mild nausea
  • constipation
  • insomnia
  • decreased sex drive
  • impotence

Warnings & Recalls for Pristiq

  • Patients taking Pristiq should avoid drinking alcohol and exposure to sunlight or tanning beds.
  • Pristiq may cause an increase in suicidal thoughts in the initial stages of use. If these symptoms worsen, tell your doctor immediately.
  • Those with a history of high blood pressure, heart problems, stroke, high cholesterol, glaucoma, kidney or liver problems, bleeding problems, or mania should alert their healthcare professionals before taking Pristiq.
  • Pristiq has received a pregnancy Category C, and should not be taken in the third trimester or later of a pregnancy, and should not be used by those who are breastfeeding.

Pristiq Treatment and Use

Pristiq | Desvenlafaxine is a selective serotonin and norepinephrone reuptake inhibitor (SNRI) manufactured by Pfizer as an extended release tablet. Pristiq is an antidepressant which works to increase levels of serotonin and norepinephrine in the brain to promote a healthy mental balance.
