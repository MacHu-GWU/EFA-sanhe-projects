Topamax Side Effects

Topamax side effects and adverse reactions include:
  • Sleepiness
  • Fatigue
  • Pins and needles
  • Memory Loss
  • Aphasia
  • Vision Problems
  • Déjà vu
  • Palinopsia and Alice In Wonderland Syndrome
  • Tongue paralysis
If you or someone you know has experienced any negative Topamax side effects, be sure to report a bad drug. Doing so helps to inform other patients of the possible risks associated with this and other drugs.

Warnings & Recalls for Topamax

  • In 2011 the FDA issued a letter to Ortho-McNeil Pharmaceuticals, Inc., that warned of the development of acute myopia and secondary angular closure glaucoma. In essence, the FDA found evidence that taking Topamax might, in some rare instances, cause severe and permanent vision loss.
  • In the same year, the FDA issued another warning to Ortho-McNeil, this time of the increased risk of cleft palate. Women of childrearing age are urged to discuss this issue with their doctors if considering treatment with Topamax.
  • Topamax side effects also contain a weight loss component, as well as the development of a notable lack of sweating and an increase in body temperature, possibly a very serious condition especially for children and the elderly.

Topamax Treatment and Use

Topamax | topiramate, is an anti-seizure medication that is also used to prevent migraine headaches, but doesn’t treat the pain associated with migraines. No one really knows for sure how this medication stops migraines or suppresses seizures, though it is believed that Topamax calms overly excitable brain cells from sending out the signals that cause these complications.

Off-label, Topamax is used for bipolar disorder, eating disorders, weight loss, and alcoholism.

Topamax Dosage

Your doctor will take many things into account before beginning you on a Topamax treatment regimen. This will include your age, the condition being treated, your weight, and other factors.

For migraines, the recommended Topamax dosage is 50 mg, twice a day. For seizure disorder, the Topamax dosage is 200 mg, twice daily. It is recommended that the dose be started much lower and gradually increased to avoid adverse Topamax side effects.
