Afinitor Side Effects

Serious Side Effects of Afinitor

Seek medical attention or consult your doctor if you experience any of these serious side effects
  • Hives, or itching, swelling of the eyes, face, mouth, tongue, etc.
  • Fast or irregular heartbeat
  • Pale skin
  • Lack of immunity to infection, disease
  • Non-infectious pneumonia
  • Seizure
  • Advanced cancer
Common Side Effects
  • Acne
  • Changes in personality
  • Change in your abilities to taste food

Warnings & Recalls for Afinitor

There is a Black Box Warning for Afinitor Taking Afinitor | Everolimus might weaken your bodies immune system, making it difficult to fight infection from viruses and more. Because of this, there is a risk of higher susceptibility to serious of life threatening diseases.Tell your doctor of any previously existing medical conditions, like Hepatitis B, before taking Afinitor.  

Afinitor Treatment and Use


Votrient | Everolimus | Afinitor  is used to treat advanced stages of renal cell carcinoma, a cancer that starts in the kidneys, if other treatments or medications have failed. It is also used in the treatment of a type of brian tumor that cannot be treated with surgery. In conjunction with other medications, Votrient | Everolimus is used in the prevention of transplant rejection, for kidney transplant patients.

 

How it Works

Afinitor | Everolimus fights cancer by stopping or slowing the cancer cells from spreading, as well as cutting off the blood supply to the cells with cancer. It also decrease immune system activity.

 

Dosage Information

Talk to your doctor to find out if Afinitor is right for you. Afinitor is taken in tablet form, by mouth, once a day. If used in the prevention of transplant rejection, Afinitor is taken up to two times a day, or every 12 hours. The drug should be taken at the same time, the same way, either with food always, or without food always.

Consult your doctor before making alterations in your dosage.  You may stop treatment if you experience threatening side effects.
