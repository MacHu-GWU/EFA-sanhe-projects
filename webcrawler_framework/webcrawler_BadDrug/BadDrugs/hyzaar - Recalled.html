Hyzaar Side Effects

Serious Side Effects of Hyzaar

Hyzaar | Losartan and hydrochlorothiazide has many serious adverse side effects associated with it such as:
  • Ophthalmic side effects such as acute angle-closure glaucoma and acute nearsightedness
  • Angioedema

Common Side Effects of Hyzaar

Less severe and more common adverse side effects are:
  • Endocrine metabolic side effects such as abnormal electrolytes
  • Musculoskeletal side effects such as backaches
  • Neurological side effects such as dizziness
  • Respiratory side effects such as upper respiratory infection

Warnings & Recalls for Hyzaar

Hyzaar Black Box Warning

Hyzaar | Losartan and hydrochlorothiazide has received a black box warning for its interaction with medications that act directly on the renin-angiotensin system can cause injury or even death to the developing fetus when used during the 2nd or 3rd trimesters of pregnancy.  Therapy with Hyzaar | Losartan and hydrochlorothiazide should be stopped immediately upon knowledge of pregnancy.  This drug’s pregnancy categories range from “C” in the 1st trimester, and “D” for both the 2nd and 3rd trimesters, according to the data published by the FDA.

Contradindications of Hyzaar

Hyzaar is contraindicated for use in patients who suffer from the following conditions:
  •  Anuria, or lack of urine
  • Hypersensitivity to any component of the product
  • Hypersensitivity to sulfonamide-derived drugs
Some warnings and precautions before taking Hyzaar | Losartan/hydrochlorothiazide are:
  • Allergy or bronchial asthma patients are at an increased risk of hypersensitivity to hydrochlorothiazide
  • Severe cirrhosis which can lead to increased risk of hypokalemia
  • Concurrent lithium therapy
  • Increased risk of latent diabetes and hyperglycemia due to diabetes mellitus
  • Brisk diuresis which leads to an increased risk of hypokalemia
  • Risk of dilutional hyponatremia in edematous patients in warm weather
  • Patients with electrolyte or fluid imbalance (hypokalemia, hyponatremia, hypomagnesemia, hypercalcemia, hypochloremic alkalosis may occur, especially with excessive vomiting or concurrent parenteral fluids)
  • Hepatic impairment or progressive liver disease (minor changes in fluid and electrolyte balance may precipitate hepatic coma)
  • Hyperuricemia or precipitation of gout may occur
  • Post-sympathectomy patients may experience antihypertensive effect enhancement
  • Prolonged therapy may increase risk of hypokalemia
  • Renal artery stenosis, unilateral or bilateral, can result in increased serum creatinine or BUN levels
  • Severe renal impairment may precipitate azotemia and cause cumulative adverse events
  • Renal function dependent on renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system patients may experience oliguria, progressive azotemia, or (rarely) acute renal failure or possibly death
  • Systemic lupus erythematosus patients may experience activation or exacerbation during thiazide diuretic therapy
  • Volume depletion and intravascular patients with diuretic treatment are at an increased risk for symptomatic hypotension

Hyzaar Treatment and Use

Hyzaar | Losartan and hydrochlorothiazide is a combination drug that is used as an antihypertensive agent, AND consists of a combination of losartan and hydrochlorothiazide.  Losartan is an angiotensin II receptor antagonist and hydrochlorothiazide is a diuretic.  This drug comes in the form of a pill.

Hyzaar | Losartan/hydrochlorothiazide, which is prepared and marketed by Merck, has FDA-approved indications for:

  • Cerebrovascular accidents in hypertensive patients with left ventricular hypertrophy
  • Hypertension (regular or severe)

The non-FDA labeled indication for this drug is for treating high blood pressure as a result of diabetes mellitus.

How Does Hyzaar Work?

Hyzaar works to lower blood pressure through the combined efforts of two medications. Losartan is an angiotensin II receptor antagonist that prevents blood vessels from constricting, while hydrochlorothiazide is a thiazide diuretic which works to prevent fluid retention by stopping the accumulation of salt in the body.

Hyzaar Dosage Information

The dosage of Hyzaar that a patient receives is individualized based on the effectiveness of the medication, as well as the appearance of side effects. The following dosages and administrations are suggested for the following conditions:

Hypertension

  • Typical initial dose of Losartan begins at 50 mg/daily, and hydrochlorothiazide  12.5 to 50 mg/daily; doses can of Hyzaar can be given in doses of 12.5 to 25 mg

Severe Hypertension

  • Initial dose of one 50/12.5 mg tablet once daily, with an increase to one table of 100/25 mg one daily after 2 -4 weeks if the smaller dose is ineffective

Patients with Hypertension who suffer from Left Ventricular Hypertrophy

  • Treatment should be combined with Cozaar 50 mg/daily; 12.5 mg of hydrochlorothiazide should be added or 50/12.5 Hyzaar should replace Cozaar if initial dosage is ineffective
  • if additional treatment is required 100 mg Cozaar and 12.5 mg of hydrochlorothiazide or 100/12.5 mg Hyzaar may be used as a replacement

Hyzaar can be taken with or without other antihypertensive medications, and with or without food.
