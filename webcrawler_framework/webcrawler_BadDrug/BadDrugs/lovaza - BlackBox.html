Lovaza Side Effects

Side Effects:

Common side effects include burping, heartburn, nausea, back pain, and a change in the way things taste. 

Serious Side Effects:

  • Chest pain

Warnings & Recalls for Lovaza

No Black Box Warnings have been recorded for this drug

Lovaza Treatment and Use

Lovaza | Omega-3-acid Ethyl Esters are used to reduce triglycerides, a fatty substance, in your blood stream. It comes in a liquid-filled gel capsule to take orally, with or without food, twice a day. If possible, take Lovaza around the same time each day. Follow directions given by your doctor.

 

How it Works:

Lovaza works by decreasing the level of triglycerides or other fat substances made by the liver. Lovaza is an antilipemic or lipid-regulating medication.
