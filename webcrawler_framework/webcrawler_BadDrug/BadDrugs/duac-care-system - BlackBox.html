Duac Care System Side Effects

Serious Side Effects of Duac Care System

  • irritated, red, burning or blistering skin
  • diarrhea with blood or water in it (could be signs of a new infection)
  • severe allergic reaction: rash, hives or reddened skin, difficulty breathing, swelling of the mouth, lips, face, eyes, tongue or throat

Common Side Effects of Duac Care System

  • minor irritation, burning, or redness of the skin
  • peeling or dryness of skin

Warnings & Recalls for Duac Care System

Duac Care System and Pregnancy

Duac Care System is listed as an FDA pregnancy Category C medication. Duac Care System's effect on the fetus when taking by a pregnant woman is unknown. It is also not known if this drug can be passed through breast-milk or harm a nursing baby. If you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or may become pregnant, talk to your doctor before using Duac Care System as a treatment.

General Warnings

Duac Care System is not for everyone; ask your doctor if it is safe to use this medicine if you have any of the following:
  • enteritis (swelling of the intestines)
  • ulcerative colitis
  • severe diarrhea induced by antibiotics
Duac Care System may make your skin more sensitive to sun and you may burn more easily. To prevent harm to the skin, avoid using tanning beds or being exposed to sunlight.Do not use sunscreen that contains para-aminobenzoic acid (PABA) while you are treating your acne with Duac Care System. When combined, these medications may cause discoloration of the skin.

Duac Care System Treatment and Use

Duac Care System | benzoyl peroxide and clindamycin topical is an antibacterial and cleansing medicine marketed by GlaxoSmithKline for treating and preventing acne.

How Does Duac Care System Work?

Duac Care System works through the combination of benzoyl peroxide and clindamycin topical. Benzoyl peroxide dries the skin and helps to remove dirt and excess oils from the face. Clindamycin prevents bacterial growth on the skin. Through cleansing the face and washing away particles that may clog pores, Duac Care System treats and reduces acne.

How to Use Duac Care System

Use Duac Care System only as recommended by your doctor. Do not use more than prescribed or for longer than instructed. Duac Care System comes in the form of a topical gel. To use Duac Care System:

  • wash hands thoroughly
  • rinse and clean your face using a mild cleanser. Do not use soap, this may leave residue on the skin.
  • with a towel, gently pat the skin until dry
  • apply to the skin twice a day (or as prescribed)
  • do not apply around the eyes, mouth or in the nose; do not use Duac Care System on skin that is broken, burned or peeling, excessively dried or irritated
  • continue use until your treatment prescription cycle has ended

Duac Care System may take many weeks to reduce your acne. Do not stop using Duac Care System unless your doctor has instructed you to do so.

Avoid contact of Duac Care System and hair or fabrics, this medicine may bleach these items.
