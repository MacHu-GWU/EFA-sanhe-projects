Xopenex Side Effects

Xopenex | Levalbuterol oral inhalation has been shown to cause side effects such as
  • dizziness
  • tremor
  • nervousness
  • anxiety
  • headache
  • cough or runny nose
  • upset stomach
  • leg cramps

Warnings & Recalls for Xopenex

  • You should not take a higher dosage than necessary of Xopenex, as fatalities have been reported with over-use of this medication.
  • Xopenex has received a pregnancy Category C label and should not be taken by those who are pregnant or breastfeeding without first consulting a doctor.

Xopenex Treatment and Use

Xopenex | Levalbuterol oral inhalation is a beta agonist manufactured by Sunovion Pharmaceuticals as a solution which is inhaled through a nebulizer. Xopenex works to make breathing easier by relaxing the muscles in the airways of the lungs.

Xopenex | Levalbuterol oral inhalation has been approved for the treatment of lung diseases such as

  • Asthma
  • Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
  • Bronchitis
  • Emphysema

Xopenex is used to treat the symptoms of these conditions, such as

  • coughing
  • wheezing
  • tightness in the chest
  • shortness of breath

Adults, adolescents, and children 4 years of age or older are all indicated for the treatment of Xopenex aerosol, while adults, adolescents, and children 6 years of age or older are indicated for the solution.
