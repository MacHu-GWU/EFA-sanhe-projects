Dexmethylphenidate Side Effects

Common Side Effects:
  • Stomach pain
  • Loss of hunger
  • Dizziness
  • Difficulty falling asleep

Serious Side Effects:

  • Chest pain
  • Slow or difficult speech
  • Weakness or numbness of arms or legs
  • Seizures
  • Believing in false things
  •  Mood changes, depression
  • Rash

Warnings & Recalls for Dexmethylphenidate

WARNING:This drug is habit forming. Do not take more than is prescribed to you by your doctor.

Dexmethylphenidate Treatment and Use

Dexmethylphenidate is used in the treatment of and to  control symptoms of attention deficit hyperactivity disorder. Dexmethylphenidate helps maintaining focus, controlling action, and staying still. Dexmethylphenidate is a medication known as a central nervous system stimulant.

How it Works: 

Dexmethylphenidate works by increasing the amounts of certain naturally forming cerebral substances, which help to lessen the effects of ADHD. It comes in tablet form, and is taken orally, typically twice a day.
