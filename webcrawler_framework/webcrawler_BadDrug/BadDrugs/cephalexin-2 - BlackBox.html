Cephalexin Side Effects

Side Effects:Seek medical help if you begin to experience any of these signs of an allergic reaction to Cephalexin:
  •  Hives
  • Swelling of the face, tongue, or throat
  • Vaginal itching or discharge
 Serious Side Effects:
  • Seizures
  • Dark colored urine
  • Bloody stool

Warnings & Recalls for Cephalexin

Warnings:Do not use Cephalexin if you are allergic to penicillin. Allergic reactions, including anaphylaxis, have been reported with penicillin and cephalosporin therapy; increased risk in patients with history of allergies, especially drug allergies. 

Cephalexin Treatment and Use

Cephalexin | is a cephalosporin antibiotic, and is used in the treatment of certain kinds of infections that are caused by bacterias like pneumonia and bone infections, ear infections, and skin or urinary tract infections.

 

Warning: Do not use Cephalexin if you are allergic to cephalexin, the active ingredient, or to other similar antibiotics like Ceclor, Ceftin, Cefzil, Duricef, Fortaz, Omnicef, Spectracef, Suprax, or others. Before using Cephalexin, inform your doctor of any allergies to drugs like penicillins, or if you have kidney or liver disease, a stomach or intestinal disorders, diabetes, or if you are malnourished. Do not stop taking this medication once prescribed, unless instructed to do so by your doctor. Symptoms may get better before the infection is completely treated.

 

Dosage

Dosing for Cephalexin varies widely from case to case. Ask your doctor what dosage is right for you and your symptoms.
