Aciphex Side Effects

Serious Side Effects of Aciphex

  • Potential risk for allergic reaction, which is characterized by swelling of the face or throat, trouble breathing, and rash
  • Continuous diarrhea may be a sign of an increased production of certain bacteria colon
  • Seizures, dizziness, involuntary muscle spasms, and irregular heartbeat  which can be the signs of a rare, but serious, condition known as hypomagnesemia
If you experience any of these serious side effects, contact your doctor immediately.

Warnings & Recalls for Aciphex

  • Proton pump inhibitor (PPI) medications have been linked with an increased risk of Clostridium difficile-associated diarrhea (CDAD), which is characterized by diarrhea that does not improve.
  • Long-term users of Aciphex face an increased risk for bone fractures associated with osteoporosis. These fractures are more common in the hip, wrist, and spine.
  • Do not take a greater dosage of Aciphex than your doctor has prescribed, as this can result in a dangerous overdose. This can result in symptoms such as watery diarrhea, difficulty breathing, convulsions, tremor, and in more serious cases, coma.

Aciphex Treatment and Use

Aciphex | Rabeprazole is a delayed-release capsule manufactured by Janssen Pharmaceuticals that comes from a class of medications known as proton pump inhibitors (PPIs). Aciphex works to reduce the levels of acid in the stomach by inhibiting the action of proton pumps that are found in acid-producing cells in the lining of the stomach.

Aciphex | Rebeprazole has been approved for the treatment of:

  • Duodenal ulcers by reducing the amount of the Heliobacter pylori bacteria found in the stomach
  • Conditions which cause an overproduction of stomach acid, such as Zollinger-Ellison syndrome
  • Ulcerative, erosive, and symptomatic Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD)

Aciphex is also used to reverse the effects of erosive esophagitis, and to reverse the damage caused by stomach acid to the esophagus.

What is Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD)?

Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) is a condition that occurs when the contents of your stomach are reversed back into the esophagus, causing the esophagus to become irritated. This can cause symptoms such as heartburn, vomiting, nausea, and damage to the esophagus.

Aciphex is able to treat this condition by decreasing the acidic content of the stomach and allowing the esophagus to heal.
