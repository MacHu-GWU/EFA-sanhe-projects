Humulin N Side Effects

This medication is designed to cause changes in the blood sugar. Be aware of the symptoms of low and high blood sugar.You may experience hypoglycemia symptoms such as:
  • dizziness
  • sweating
  • lightheadedness
  • pale skin
  • numbness
  • weakness
If you do not treat these symptoms of hypoglycemia, you may experience severe symptoms:
  • seizures
  • loss of consciousness
 

SEVERE SIDE EFFECTS:

Seek medical attention if you experience any of the symptoms of high blood sugar
  • Dry mouth
  • Frequent urination
  • Blurred vision
Side effects of Insulin:Call your doctor immediately if you have these symptoms
  • Wheezing
  • Itch or rash over the entire body
  • Fast heartbeat
  • Difficulty breathing

Humulin N Treatment and Use

Humulin N is an Insulin Injection, used in the regulation of blood sugar for people with Type 1 Diabetes or Type 2 Diabetes. It is a hormone medication, used as a replacement for the insulin that is normally produced in the body.

How it works:

Humulin N works by taking sugar out of the blood and putting it into other body areas where it can be used as energy. Insulin also keeps the liver from creating any more sugar. All types of insulin work like this, but some work faster than others, depending on the needs of the injector and the types of insulin. Insulin does not cure diabetes.

Dosing Information:

Ask your doctor what dosage is right for you.
