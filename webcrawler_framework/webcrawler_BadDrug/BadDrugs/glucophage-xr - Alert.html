Glucophage XR Side Effects

Serious Side Effects:

  • Allergic Reactions such as Rash outbreak
  • Troubled Breathing
  • Swelling of the limbs or face
  • Weakness
  • Discomfort
 Common Side Effects: 
  • Gas
  • Indigestion
  • Stomach pain or diarrhea
 

Warnings & Recalls for Glucophage XR

Glucophage XR may cause a fatal condition called lactic acidosis. If you have liver or kidney problems, are elderly or drink alcohol, the risk is greater. Talk to your doctor about his condition and see if Glucophage XR is right for you.  

Glucophage XR Treatment and Use

Glucophage XR | Metformin is an extended release tablet used in the treatment of Type 2 Diabetes. Used in combination with a doctor approved diet and exercise regimen, Glucophage XR can help treat Type 2 Diabetes symptoms. Glucophage XR is in a class of medications called biguanide anti-diabetics.

 

How it Works:

Glucophage XR is a biguanide anti-diabetic. It works by diminishing the amount of sugars that the liver creates. It also decreases the amount of sugar the intestines absorb. It helps to increase the body’s sensitivity to naturally produced insulin.

 

Dosing Information:

Talk to your doctor about what dosage is right for you.
