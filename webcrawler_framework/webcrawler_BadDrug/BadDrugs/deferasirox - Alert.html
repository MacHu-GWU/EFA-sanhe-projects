Deferasirox Side Effects

Common Side Effects:
  • Stomach pain
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea

Serious Side Effects:

  • Hearing loss
  • Vision problems
  • Hives or rash
  • Hoarseness
  • Swollen face or tongue
 

Warnings & Recalls for Deferasirox

Deferasirox may cause serious or life-threatening damage to the kidneys or liver or severe bleeding in the stomach or intestines. The risk that you will develop these conditions is greater if you are elderly or if you have any of the following conditions: high risk myelodysplastic syndrome, a low level of platelets, or kidney or liver disease.

Deferasirox Treatment and Use

Deferasirox is used to remove iron storage excess in the bodies of people who received a large number of transfusions of blood. Deferasirox is a medication known as iron chelators.

How it Works:

Deferasirox works through attaching to the iron in a body, so that the excess iron is excreted in the feces.
