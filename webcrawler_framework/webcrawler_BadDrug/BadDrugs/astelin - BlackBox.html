Astelin Side Effects

Serious Side Effects of Astelin

  • Discontinue taking Astelin and get medical attention if ou start to experience unexplained shortness of breath,wheezing, or  a tight feelingin your chest,.
  • If your pulse begins to become uneven or faster than usual, stop using Astelin and talk to your doctor as soon as possible.

Common Side Effects of Astelin

  • After using Astelin, you may notice a bitter taste or dryness in your mouth, a sore throat or nasal burning.
  • Astelin can make you feel drowsy. Do not perform any activities that require your complete attention, such as driving, until you know how Astelin will affect you. Avoid drinking while taking Astelin. It can increase the drowsy effects of this medication.
  • You may experience  reddened eyes, a headache, feel nausea, or become dizzy after using Astelin.
  • Astelin may cause you to gain weight.

Warnings & Recalls for Astelin

Astelin Warnings

  • Make sure to talk to your doctor about other medications you are taking. Including medicines for colds and allergies, any seizure medications, drugs to treat depression or anxiety, antihistamines, sleeping pills, pain medication, or vitamins.
  • If a child consumes Astelin by accident, immediately call the poison control center or your doctor.
  • Do not spray Astelin into your eyes or mouth.
  • If your symptoms do not get better, discuss alternatives with your doctor.
  • Discuss any history of kidney problems with your doctor.
  • If you are pregnant, breast feeding, or planning on becoming pregnant, talk to your doctor to see if you can safely take Astelin.

Astelin Treatment and Use

Astelin | Azelastine Hydrochloride is used as a treatment for allergy symptoms. These symptoms include a running, itching, or stuffed nose, post-nasal drip, and frequent sneezing. this medication can treat the symptoms of both seasonal and nonseasonal allergies. It can also sometimes be used to treat the symptoms of hay fever.

How does Astelin Work?

Astelin is an antihistamine. Antihistamines work within your immune system to block the compounds in your body that cause allergic reactions. By blocking these compounds, antihistamines can reduce common symptoms of allergies.

Astelin Dosage Information

Astelin is a nasal spray that is typically used once or twice per nostril each day. Your doctor may change your dosage based on you medical condition, the strength of the medication, and any previous response to a similar treatment. Astelin typically will begin to work three hours after use.

To experience the highest benefit, use Astelin on a regular basis by spraying the medication around the same time every day.

You should not use more or less of the product than your doctor has prescribed you to take.
