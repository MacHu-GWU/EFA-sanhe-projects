Viracept Side Effects

Serious Side Effects:

If you experience any of these side effects, seek medical attention immediately.
  • Sudden hives breakout
  • Troubled breathing and swallowing
  • Strange itching
 Common Side Effects:
  • Diarrhea and nausea
  • Increase in gas
  • A loss of hunger

Warnings & Recalls for Viracept

There are no Black Box Warnings recorded for this medication.

Viracept Treatment and Use

Viracept | Nelfinavir is used in a combination with other medications in the treatment of human immunodeficiency virus (HIV). Viracept is a protease inhibitor medication.

 

How it Works: 

Viracept decreases the amount of the human immunodeficiency virus in the blood stream, but does not cure HIV. It may, however, decrease the likeliness of patients suffering from HIV from acquiring acquired immunodeficiency syndrome or other HIV related illness, such as cancer or other infection. Using this medication in a combination of safer sex habits, may even decrease the risk of transmitting HIV to others.

 

Dosing Information:

Ask your doctor what dosing is right for you.
