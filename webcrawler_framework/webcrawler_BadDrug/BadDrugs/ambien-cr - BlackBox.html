Ambien CR Treatment and Use

Ambien CR | zolpidem is a sedating, extended release hypnotic medication that is indicated for the short-term treatment of insomnia in those who have difficulty falling or staying asleep. Off label this drug is also used to treat certain brain disorders. Brain imaging studies have shown that in certain instances, damaged parts of the brain began functioning after treatment with zolpidem, although there are no universally recognized off-label uses for Ambien CR.

How does Ambien CR work?

Ambien is part of a class of drugs called hypnotics. These drugs act on chemicals in the brain that may cause agitation and anxiety that may lead to insomnia.

Ambien CR dosage

  • Ambien CR | zolpidem comes as an extended release tablet that is to be taken orally and without food.
  • You should take Ambien CR for your insomnia right before bedtime.
  • Do not crush or dissolve tablets before taking.
  • Usual adult dosage of Ambien CR for insomnia is 12.5 mg.
  • Those who are elderly, may have another underlying medical condition, or who have liver disease should take a decreased dose.
