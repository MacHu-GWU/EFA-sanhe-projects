Clarithromycin Side Effects

Common Side Effects:
  • Gas
  • Change in Taste
  • Headache
  • Vomiting

Serious Side Effects:

  • Rash
  • Peeling or blistering skin
  • Fever
  • Yellow skin
  • Extreme Tiredness
  • Dark Colored Urine
  • Unusual Bleeding

Warnings & Recalls for Clarithromycin

There are no black box warnings recorded for this drug.

Clarithromycin Treatment and Use

Clarithromycin is used to treat bacterial infections, like pneumonia, bronchitis, and of the ears, skin, sinuses, and even the throat. It can even be used to treat or prevent disseminated Mycobacterium avium complex infection, which is a type of lung infection for people with HIV.
