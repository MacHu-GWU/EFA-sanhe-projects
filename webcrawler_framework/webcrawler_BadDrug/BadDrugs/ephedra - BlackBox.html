Serious Side Effects of Ephedra

Ephedra has been banned for its serious side effects which include:
  • Death caused by complications from the drug
  • Heart damage
  • Increased rate of heat stroke
  • Kidney stones
  • Tremors
 Ephedra should not be used if pregnant or nursing. Ephedra should not be ingested within a week or two of a surgery.Ephedra is banned in America.

Ephedra Treatment and Use

Ephedra Treatment and Use

Ephedra is an herbal supplement used for weight loss, although the herb itself has been used in conjunction with other herbs as a remedy for cold.

 

How does Ephedra Work?

Ephedra, as it is commonly known, is a combination of two plant alkaloids, ephedrine and psuedophedrine, which are said to stimulate the brain when ingested, and thus stimulate various body functions, such as the opening and the closing of the lungs, or the increase in heart rate.

Because Ephedra stimulates certain brain receptors, like the ones that control your heart, it has been flagged by the FDA, and is currently banned in the U.S.A.
