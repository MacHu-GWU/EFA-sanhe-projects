Feldene Side Effects

Serious Adverse Side Effects

Feldene | Piroxicam has some very serious adverse side effects associated with it that can be quite hazardous, such as:
  • Cardiovascular side effects such as congestive heart failure, heart attacks and other cardiovascular events
  • Dermatological side effects such as erythroderma (an inflammatory skin disease), Stevens-Johnson syndrome, and toxic epidermal necrolysis, two deadly allergic skin reactions
  • Endocrine metabolic side effects such as high blood potassium
  • Gastrointestinal side effects such as intestinal hemorrhage, perforation of stomach, ulcers, inflammatory disorder of the digestive tract and pancreatic inflammation
  • Hematological side effects such as lowered white blood cell count, lowered blood cell counts, anemia, and decrease of platelets in the blood
  • Hepatic side effects such as the wasting of the liver, jaundice and liver failure
  • Immunological side effects such as anaphlyactoid reactions, a deadly allergic reaction
  • Neurological side effects such as stroke
  • Renal side effects such as kidney failure
  • Respiratory side effects such as constriction of the airways
  • Other side effects such as transfusion reaction

Common Adverse Side Effects

Other more common and less severe adverse side effects of this drug include:
  • Cardiovascular side effects such as edema, or abnormal fluid accumulation underneath the skin
  • Dermatological side effects such as itching and rash
  • Gastrointestinal side effects such as abdominal pain, constipation, diarrhea, flatulence, heartburn, indigestion, loss of appetite, nausea and vomiting
  • Hematological side effects such as anemia
  • Hepatic side effects such as increased liver functioning
  • Neurological side effects such as dizziness and headache
  • Otic side effects such as tinnitus, a condition where hearing is impacted
  • Renal side effects such as abnormal kidney function

Warnings & Recalls for Feldene

Black Box Warning

Feldene | Piroxicam has the following “black box” warning associated with it, denoting that NSAIDs can cause increased risks of serious cardiovascular events such as heart attacks and stroke which can be fatal.  This risk is especially heightened in patients with an established cardiovascular history.  NSAIDs are also deadly in elderly patients as there’s a risk for gastrointestinal side effects such as bleeding, perforation of the stomach and ulcers which can prove to be fatal.

Contraindications

Feldene | Piroxicam is contraindicated in the following patients:
  • Asthma, urticarial and allergic-type reactions have occurred following giving the patient aspirin
  • Patients who have undergone coronary artery bypass graft surgery
  • Patients with a hypersensitivity to Piroxicam

Who Should Not Take This Drug

The following patients should never take Feldene | Piroxicam under any circumstances:
  • Patients with a cardiovascular history
  • Elderly patients
  • Patients with asthma
  • Patients with blood coagulation disorders
  • Patients prescribed this medication are advised to take it for a short duration
  • Patients with abnormal liver function
  • Patients with hypertension
  • Patients with peptic ulcer disease
  • Patients with kidney disorders or impairments

Feldene Treatment and Use

Feldene | Piroxicam is a drug whose classification is that of a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) of the oxicam class.  The FDA-labeled indications for this drug are meant to treat osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis.  Non-FDA labeled indications that are treated with Feldene | Piroxicam comprise of adjunct cancer pain, dental pain, dysmenorrhea, acute gouty arthritis, post-episiotomy pain, pain after operations, and soft tissue injury.  This drug comes in pill form.

Feldene | Piroxicam is manufactured and marketed by Pfizer in the US.  It is worth noting that this drug is also used in veterinary medicine to treat certain tumors from animals such as colon and bladder tumors and also prostate cancers.

Please refer to the “warnings and recalls” tab to get more information on the “black box” warning associated with Feldene | Piroxicam.

How This Drug Works

The mechanism of action for Feldene | Piroxicam is that its status as a NSAID drug is thought to stop the body’s production of prostaglandin synthetase, a substance that causes fever, pain and inflammation.

Dosage Information

With the administration of Feldene | Piroxicam, it is important to use the lowest and yet most effective dose for the shortest possible duration.  A physician will monitor a patient extensively to observe their initial response, to possibly adjust the dosage and frequency with which a patient is given this drug to take.  This information on dosing is meant to be exclusively for this drug’s FDA-labeled indications, not its non-FDA labeled ones.

  • For osteoarthritis, 20mg per day orally
  • For rheumatoid arthritis, 20mg per day orally
