Hectorol Side Effects

  • Headache
  • Upset stomach
  • Constipation
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Tiredness
More serious adverse side effects include:
  • Muscle, bone or joint pain
  • Loss of appetite
  • Increased thirst
  • Increased urination
  • Abnormal or fast heartbeat
  • Mood changes
  • Metallic taste in the mouth
  • Weakness
  • Unexplainable weight loss
  • Possibility of allergic reaction

Warnings & Recalls for Hectorol

Hectorol shouldn’t be administered to patients who’ve got a tendency towards hypercalcemia or who exhibit  current evidence of vitamin D toxicity.As of yet, there hasn’t been any safety guidelines from the FDA initiated in the form of labeling changes or a recall by the FDA because Hectorol has been deemed to be an unsafe drug.  There also hasn’t been a voluntary recall by Sanofi-Aventis either.

Hectorol Treatment and Use

Hectorol | Doxercalciferol is a drug that’s meant to be taken for metabolic bone disease and hyperparathyroidism in patients with Stage 3 or 4 chronic kidney disease.  It consists of a synthetic analog of ergocalciferol (vitamin D2) and suppresses parathyroid synthesis and secretion.  Hectorol can come in injectable form or capsule form and is manufactured by Sanofi-Aventis.
