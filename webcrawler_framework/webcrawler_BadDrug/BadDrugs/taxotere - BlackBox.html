Taxotere Side Effects

Taxotere | docetaxel injection has been shown to cause side effects such as
  • weakness
  • tiredness
  • nausea
  • vomiting
  • diarrhea
  • constipation
  • loss of appetite
  • muscle pain
  • missed menstrual periods
  • temporary hair loss
  • changes in fingernails or toenails

Warnings & Recalls for Taxotere

  • Taxotere should not be used if you are pregnant, wanting to become pregnant, or breastfeeding, as it could potentially cause damage to an unborn infant.
  • Those with a history of liver disease, kidney disease, heart disease, congestive heart failure, or fluid retention problems should consult a doctor before using this medication.
  • Patients with abnormal liver function, non-small cell lung carcinoma, a history of platinum-based chemotherapy, or those who receive higher doses of Taxotere face an increased risk for treatment-related mortality while taking Taxotere
  • Severe hypersensitivity has been reported in patients receiving the suggested 3-day premedication. If you experience any symptoms relating to this condition, such as rash, hypotension, bronchospasm, or anaphylaxis, stop taking Taxotere immediately and consult with your healthcare physician before continuing dosage
