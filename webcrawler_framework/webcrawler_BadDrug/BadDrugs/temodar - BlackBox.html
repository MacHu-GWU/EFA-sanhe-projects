Temodar Side Effects

Temodar | temozolomide has been shown to cause common side effects, such as:
  • alopecia
  • fatigue
  • nausea
  • vomiting
  • headache
  • constipation
  • rash
  • hemiparesis
  • diarrhea
  • asthenia
  • fever
  • dizziness
  • lack of coordination
  • amnesia
  • insomnia
Temodar may cause more serious side effects, such as:
  • anorexia
  • seizures
  • viral infection
  • easy bruising or bleeding, such as nosebleeds or gum bleeding
  • pain or burning during urination
  • dark urine
  • jaundice
  • clay-colored stools
  • upper stomach pain
If you experience any serious side effects, or any common side effects persist, talk to your doctor immediately.

Warnings & Recalls for Temodar

Temodar should not be used in patients who have a history of hypersensitivity, or who are allergic to temozolomide or dacarbazine (DTIC-Dome).Patients who are pregnant should not use Temodar, as this medication can cause abnormalities in an unformed fetus.Temodar has been shown to cause the following rare, but serious conditions:
  • Myelosuppression, which can result in aplastic anemia and be potentially fatal
  • Myelodysplastic syndrome
  • Pneumocystis carinii pneumonia, which is more likely to occur in patients who take Temodar for an extended period of time
Patients with a history of kidney or liver disease should consult their doctor before using Temodar.
