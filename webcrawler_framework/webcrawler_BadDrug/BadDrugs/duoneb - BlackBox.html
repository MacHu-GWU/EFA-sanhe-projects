  • Swelling of body parts

DuoNeb Treatment and Use

DuoNeb | Albuterol and Ipratropium Inhalation is a combination used to help in the prevention of difficult breathing, and other symptoms of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. Symptoms of COPD include tightness of the chest, coughing, wheezing, chronic bronchitis, and emphysema.

 

How it Works: 

DuoNeb works through the combination of albuterol and ipratropium, which is used to treat patients whose symptoms cannot be controlled with one inhaled medication. DuoNeb is a bronchodilator, which relax and open air ways to the lungs, and make the act of breathing much less difficult.
