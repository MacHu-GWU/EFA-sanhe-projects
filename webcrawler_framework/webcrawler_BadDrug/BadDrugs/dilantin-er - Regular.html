Dilantin ER Side Effects

Dilantin ER | phenytoin has been shown to cause side effects such as
  • slurred speech
  • loss of balance or coordination
  • swollen or tender gums
  • headache
  • dizziness
  • nervousness
  • insomnia

Warnings & Recalls for Dilantin ER

  • Patients taking Dilantin ER should avoid consuming alcohol or taking antacids, as these can interfere with the absorption of the medication by the body, and increase risk of seizures.
  • Dilantin ER has may cause an increase in suicidal thoughts, and patients should be monitored closely while taking this medication.
  • Those who are pregnant, trying to become pregnant, or breastfeeding should not start taking Dilantin ER. However, if you become pregnant while using this medication, consult your doctor and do not stop taking Dilantin ER.

Dilantin ER Treatment and Use

Dilantin ER | phenytoin is an anticonvulsant manufactured as an extended-release capsule which works to impede impulses in the brain which can cause seizures. This prevents over-stimulation of nerves in the brain, and interferes with the transmission of impulses between nerves.

Dilantin ER | phenytoin has been approved for the treatment of

  • Epilepsy
  • Grand mal seizures
  • Complex partial seizures, such as temporal lobe seizures
